about

We offer the finest selection of flowers with creative designs and artistic touches for all occasions. Our shop takes pride in providing fresh, hand-picked flowers, from classic roses and tulips to luxurious orchids and lilies. We guarantee high quality and a unique experience with each bouquet, along with fast and exceptional delivery service. Whether it’s a celebration or a gift, we’ll help you express your feelings with elegance and care

Rose

Description:

The rose is a symbol of love and romance, available in various colors like red, white, pink, and yellow

scent:

Strong and fragrant, especially the red roses

price:

Around 10 EGP per rose

care tips:

Keep in a cool place and change water daily

Tulip

Description:

Known for its simplicity and elegance, tulips come in vibrant colors like red, yellow, and purple.

scent:

Mild or nearly scentless

price:

Approximately 15 EGP per flower

care tips:

Best in cool water, avoid direct sunlight

Lily

Description:

A luxurious flower in colors like white and pink, often used in upscale floral arrangements

scent:

Fresh and strong

price:

Around 25 EGP per flower

care tips:

Prefers indirect light and water change every two days

Orchid

Description:

Elegant and exotic, orchids are available in colors such as purple, white, and yellow

scent:

lightly fragrant

price:

Approximately 40 EGP per flower

care tips:

Needs high humidity and low light

Sunflower

Description:

A cheerful flower with bright yellow petals, bringing a touch of vibrance to any space

scent:

Almost scentless

price:

Around 20 EGP per flower

care tips:

Thrives in direct sunlight, with regular water changes.

Carnation

Description:

Long-lasting and available in multiple colors, carnations add charm and durability to bouquets

scent:

Light and fresh

price:

About 12 EGP per flower

care tips:

Change water daily and avoid high heat
